Why we started Zum

As busy working parents we faced the frustration and exhaustion of finding trustworthy, safe and reliable people to drive our kids and take care of them. So we designed an innovative service with focus on trust, safety and peace of mind that every parent and child deserves.

Why we’re different

We are a ride sharing service that sends consistent drivers for your child and an on-demand nanny for any care you may need around your child’s pick-up or drop off.

TRUST

FAMILIAR DRIVERS

Familiarity and continuity is important. We match you to 1 of 5 drivers assigned to your family anytime you book a ride with us.

CONVENIENCE

CARE SERVICES

On those days when you need more than a ride, Zum can provide childcare or can stay back with your kid at activities.

PEACE OF MIND

DEDICATED ASSISTANT

You’ll have a single point of contact who understands your family needs and can be reached at the touch of a button.
